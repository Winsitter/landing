Actually Prevent Downtime??

Yep. We alert you of many conditions before they become an outage. SSL certificate expiration, RAM/CPU/Drive capacity, disk I/O, Kerberos time checks... and more. Warnings come as emails and SMS before it becomes an outage issue.

But Why?

We love Windows. But let’s face it, it’s still difficult for busy admins to get notified of common every-day server issues.

We created Winsitter in a way that removes complexity and geekiness of our monitoring solution so anyone can signup, install the agent, and sleep better knowing they’ll be notified of issues before users notify them.
